Your success story begins here

The Japanese market is one of the most dynamic and attractive markets, but it is not an easy task to conquer it. It does not matter what your company reached and if it has the name in the world or not. The strategy that you have used earlier will not bring you any benefit in Japan. Rather on the contrary. In the best case it will slow down the promotion of the product, how it happened with Uber and FaceBook, which could not fully capture Japanese market. In the worst case you will have to cease your activity, how it happened with eBay. Other, equally well-known companies such as Amazon, Apple, Starbucks were able to stay in the Japanese market and even take a leadership position just because they have changed their strategy. They have adapted to the specificity of the Japanese market, its culture and accepted business rules. There are three fundamental conditions that are necessary for gaining success in the Japanese market:

  • Professional localization of your product (authenticity of the translation helps to avoid ambiguity and increase the level of trust);
  • Development of an effective strategy, with considering the features of a product or service;
  • Willingness to follow the rules of doing business in Japan.

Partnerships with CoinJapan is your guarantee of entering Japanese investment market for the providing of successful ICO. We will help to raise funds for your project because we understand the market, know its features, requirements and business rules. There are three components that are the basis of our confidence in the successful attraction of Japanese investment to your business:

  • Promotion of what is interesting for Japanese investors;
  • Using of methods adopted in Japanese business;
  • The growth and reputation of our company are related to the number of successful ICO

A wise decision to cooperate with CoinJapan will open your business for investment from Japan. We are sure in our capabilities and can create solutions for the growth of your business!
